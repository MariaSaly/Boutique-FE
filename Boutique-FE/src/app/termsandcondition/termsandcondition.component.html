Shipping Policy

Introduction

At SAME PINCH, we are committed to providing prompt and reliable shipping services to ensure a smooth and satisfying shopping experience for our customers. This shipping policy outlines important details regarding processing times, delivery estimates, shipping methods, and tracking information. By placing an order with us, you agree to comply with the terms and conditions set forth in this policy.

Order Processing Time

We strive to ship your products as quickly as possible. For orders within India, please allow up to 24 hours for us to process and dispatch your items. International orders require a processing time of approximately 3 days. Please note that processing times may vary during peak seasons or promotional periods.

Shipping Methods and Carriers

To ensure reliable and efficient shipping, we work with trusted shipping carriers, such as Professionals, Speed Post, and Delhivery for domestic orders within India. For international orders, we partner with India Post, DHL, and other reputable carriers. The shipping carrier for your order will be determined based on the destination and the most suitable shipping method available.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs will be calculated and displayed during the checkout process. The shipping fee is determined by factors such as weight, dimensions, destination, and the selected shipping method for your order. Please note that any applicable customs duties or taxes for international shipments are the responsibility of the recipient.

Delivery Time

For domestic orders within India, you can expect your package to be delivered within 3 to 9 days from the date of dispatch. International orders typically take between 5 to 12 days to reach their destination. Please note that delivery times may vary depending on customs clearance processes, local holidays, or unforeseen circumstances.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number via email. You can also find the tracking information on our website. Use the provided tracking number to monitor the progress of your package during transit and obtain estimated delivery dates.

Pre-Order Dispatch

For pre-orders, please allow up to 7 days for us to dispatch your items. We will keep you informed about the status of your pre-order and provide updates on the estimated dispatch date.

Contact Information

To ensure a smooth delivery process, please provide accurate contact details, including your full address and mobile number. It is crucial to double-check the information you provide during the checkout process to avoid any delivery issues or delays.

Final Note

We strive to provide excellent shipping services, and your satisfaction is our priority. If you have any further questions or concerns regarding our shipping policy, please contact our customer support team. Thank you for choosing and trusting SAME PINCH.
