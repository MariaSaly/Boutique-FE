Returns and Exchange Policy

Policy Overview

At BoutiqueApp, we strive to ensure that our customers are delighted with their purchases. If you're not satisfied with your order, we offer a simple and hassle-free returns and exchange process. Please review our policy details below to ensure a smooth experience.

Eligibility for Returns and Exchanges

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • Products should be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging with tags attached.
  • Customized or final sale items are not eligible for returns or exchanges.

How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

Follow these steps to return or exchange your item:

  1. Log in to your BoutiqueApp account.
  2. Navigate to the Order History section.
  3. Select the item you wish to return or exchange and click Request Return.
  4. Follow the instructions to print your return shipping label.
  5. Pack the item securely and attach the shipping label to the package.
  6. Drop off the package at your nearest shipping center.

Once we receive your package, our team will process your request within 5-7 business days.

Refunds

Refunds will be issued via the original payment method within 5-7 business days after the return is approved. Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part.

Exchanges

If you need a different size, color, or item, exchanges are subject to availability. We recommend initiating your request promptly to ensure the desired product is in stock.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our support team immediately at or call us at +1-234-567-8901. Include pictures of the damaged product for faster resolution.
